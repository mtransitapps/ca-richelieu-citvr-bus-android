Full description:
This application adds CIT Vallée du Richelieu buses information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule and the latest news from www.citvr.ca, YouTube and @citvrichelieu on Twitter.

CIT Vallée du Richelieu serves Belœil, McMasterville, Mont-Saint-Hilaire, Otterburn Park, Saint-Basile-le-Grand, Saint-Hyacinthe, Sainte-Madeleine and Sainte-Marie-Madeleine.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Agence Métropolitaine de Transport.
https://www.amt.qc.ca/en/about/open-data

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-richelieu-citvr-bus-android

This app is not related with the Agence Métropolitaine de Transport and CIT Vallée du Richelieu.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from www.citvr.ca, YouTube and Twitter.
