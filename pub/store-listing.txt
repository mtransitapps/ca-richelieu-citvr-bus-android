Full description:
This application adds RTM (CIT) Vallée du Richelieu buses information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule and the latest news from rtm.quebec, YouTube and @citvrichelieu on Twitter.

RTM (CIT) Vallée du Richelieu serves Belœil, McMasterville, Mont-Saint-Hilaire, Otterburn Park, Saint-Basile-le-Grand, Saint-Hyacinthe, Sainte-Madeleine and Sainte-Marie-Madeleine.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Réseau de transport métropolitain (AMT).
https://rtm.quebec/en/about/open-data

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-richelieu-citvr-bus-android

This app is not related with the Réseau de transport métropolitain (AMT) and RTM (CIT) Vallée du Richelieu.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from rtm.quebec, YouTube and Twitter.
