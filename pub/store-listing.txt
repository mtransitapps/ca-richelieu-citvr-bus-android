Full description:
This application adds exo Vallée du Richelieu buses information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule and the latest news from rtm.quebec, YouTube and @exo_VRichelieu on Twitter.

exo Vallée du Richelieu serves Belœil, McMasterville, Mont-Saint-Hilaire, Otterburn Park, Saint-Basile-le-Grand, Saint-Hyacinthe, Sainte-Madeleine and Sainte-Marie-Madeleine in Québec, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM).
https://rtm.quebec/en/about/open-data

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-richelieu-citvr-bus-android

This app is not related with the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM), the Autorité Régionale de Transport Métropolitain (ARTM) and exo Vallée du Richelieu.

exo Vallée du Richelieu was formely know as RTM Vallée du Richelieu sector and CIT Vallée du Richelieu.
exo was formely know as Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM) and Agence Métropolitaine de Transport (AMT).

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from rtm.quebec, YouTube and Twitter.
